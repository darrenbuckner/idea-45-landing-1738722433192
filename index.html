Executive Overview

Vouch aims to foster genuine, high-quality relationships through an exclusive invite-only social networking platform where members are admitted based on personal invitations and are vouched for by existing members.

Problem Statement

Mainstream social networking platforms tend to prioritize engagement and virality, often resulting in quantity-focused relationships rather than quality-focused ones.

Solution Statement

Vouch aims to solve this problem by prioritizing quality over quantity. With its invite-only and vouching system, Vouch creates a trusting environment, encouraging the formation of meaningful, professional, or personal connections.

Market Analysis
